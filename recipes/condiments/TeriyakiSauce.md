# Teriyaki Sauce

## Ingredients

- 1 c water
- 1/3 cup soy Sauce tsp brown sugar
- 1 tbs honey
- 1/2 tsp ground ginger
- 1/4 tsp garlic powder
- 2 tbs corn starch
- 1/4 c cold water

## Preparation

1. combine
  - water
  - soy Sauce
  - brown sugar
  - honey
  - ginger
  - garlic
1. simmer mixture for 1 min
1. combine cornstarch and water
1. combine slurry w/ mixture
1. cook until thickened ~5-7 minutes
