# Chocolate Chop Banana Nut Muffins

## Ingredients

- 1 3/4 c flour
- 3/4 c sugar
- 1 1/4 tsp baking powder
- 1/2 tsp baking soda
- 1/2 c nuts
- 3/4 tsp salt
- 2 eggs
- 2 brown bananas
- 1/3 c butter (melted)
- 1/3 c beer
- 3/4 c semi-sweet chocolate chips
- 1 tsp vanilla

## Preparation

1. combine wet ingredients & sugar
1. combine dry
1. add dry to wet
1. bake @ 350 for ~ 35 min
