
# Whole Wheat Beer Bread

Bread machine recipe

## Ingredients

- 2 1/2 tsp yeast
- 2 1/2 cups whole wheat flour
- 1 1/4 cup bread flour
- 1 tbs sugar
- 1 tsp salt
- 3 tbs nonfat dry milk
- 1 tbs green chili powder
- 1 tbs red chili powder

- 2 tbs butter
- 1 egg
- 12 oz beer (one can)

## Preparation

Standard bread machine cycle
