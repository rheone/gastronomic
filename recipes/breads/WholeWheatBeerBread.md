
# Wheat Beer Bread

**1.5 lb bread machine loaf**

## Ingredients

### Wet Ingredients

- 2 tbs butter
- 1 egg
- 12 oz beer (one can)

### Dry Ingredients

- 2 tsp yeast
- 2 cups whole wheat flour
- 1 1/4 cup bread flour
- 1 tbs sugar
- 1 tsp salt
- 2 tbs nonfat dry milk
- 1 tbs green chili powder
- 1 tbs red chili powder

## Preparation

Standard bread machine cycle
